ABOUT US

Welcome to the heart of student advocacy and empowerment at Ajayi Crowther University. The Student Assembly Body is a dedicated team of elected student leaders committed to amplifying the voices, interests, and concerns of the diverse student body.

As elected representatives, we serve as a vital link between students, faculty, and administration. We actively engage with our peers to understand their perspectives, gather feedback, and champion their interests across various facets of university life.
